ℹ️ ABOUT

Why CampusVoice
Exists

Students deserve a campus that works. CampusVoice gives every student a
direct, accountable channel to report issues and get them resolved.

🎯


Our Purpose


Campus maintenance complaints often go unheard. CampusVoice creates a transparent, trackable system where every complaint is logged, assigned, and resolved — with full visibility to the student who filed it.

🔍


Why Complaints Matter


A broken projector disrupts a lecture. A faulty water tap affects hundreds. By collecting structured complaint data, administration can identify recurring issues, prioritize fixes, and allocate maintenance resources smarter.

🏛️


Who It Helps


Students get a voice. Administration gets organized, prioritized feedback. Maintenance teams get clear work orders with photos and locations. Everyone wins when communication is streamlined.

📊


Tracked & Transparent


Every complaint moves through a clear pipeline: Pending → In Progress → Resolved. No more wondering if your report was seen. No more issues falling through the cracks.

🔄 PROCESS FLOW

From Complaint to Resolution

1

Student Submits Complaint

The student fills out the complaint form — name, ID, location, category, description, and optional photo. Submission takes under 2 minutes.

2

Admin Receives & Reviews

The college administration is immediately notified. The complaint is categorized and assigned to the relevant department (electrical, plumbing, IT, etc.).

3

Maintenance Team Dispatched

The relevant maintenance team is briefed and dispatched to the location. The complaint status updates to "In Progress".

4

Problem Resolved & Confirmed

Once the issue is fixed, admin marks it "Resolved". The student can confirm on the complaints board that their issue has been addressed.
