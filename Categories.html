🗂️ SERVICES

Complaint
Categories

Choose from six infrastructure categories. Each complaint is routed to
the right department for fast resolution.

⚡

Electricity

Report broken lights, power outages, non-functional switches, overloaded sockets, or any electrical safety hazard across campus buildings.

📶

Internet / WiFi

Dead zones, slow connectivity, disconnected access points, or network outages affecting academic work — report it and get it fixed.

🚰

Water

Report water leakages, no water supply, broken taps, clogged drains, or washroom water issues. Quick fixes prevent damage to infrastructure.

🧹

Cleanliness

Unclean classrooms, dirty washrooms, littered corridors, overflowing dustbins, or pest issues that affect the campus environment.

🪑

Classroom Issues

Broken furniture, non-working ACs or fans, damaged projectors, missing whiteboards — anything that disrupts the learning environment.

📚

Library Problems

Book unavailability, broken study carrels, non-working computers, restricted access, or noise issues in the library zone.
